Profile

My name is Patrik, I am a Web Publisher and front-end developer from Berne, Switzerland.
I have worked with 30+ clients all over the world, small and large. Clean, simple and usable design with attention to details is my trade mark.

I love beautifully crafted designs, for print and web. Fuelled by all kinds of indie and electronic music I can tinker for days. Hence, I like to think that portefeuille will help others in creating an online portfolio, too.
